Frequently Asked Questions:

What if there is bad weather on the day of my party?

Our Mobile Game Theaters come with heating and air conditioning so that the party can continue no matter what the weather is like outside. As long as weather conditions permit road travel we will be there.

Can we eat and drink inside the Mobile Gaming Theater?

We ask that guests refrain from eating inside the theater and focus on gaming instead.

How do I know that the Mobile Game Theater can come to my house?

We have excellent drivers and are able to park nearly anywhere. As long as your driveway or street isn't on a steep hill, we'll be able to come! Otherwise, we can try to find a secondary location such as a park or church with flat ground.

What time will you arrive for the party?

We arrive about 20 minutes before our scheduled time to set up.

How should I set the itinerary for my party?

We recommend setting the start of your party 30 minutes to an hour before our scheduled arrival to make sure that all of your guests have arrived. Then they get to see us pull up with the Mobile Gaming Theater!

Should I be concerned about the types of games for kids?

While booking your party, please let us know of any game concerns and we can address them on an individual basis. You can also communiate with the game coaches during the party regarding any concerns.

Do I have to pay for the party in advance?

You must pay a 30% deposit online to reserve your date and time.

Do I need to tip the game coaches?

Game coaches are there to make sure that your party has maximum fun! Tipping is not required, but greatly appreciated.

How do the invitations work?

We will mail the invitations to you after you pay your deposit. Unless you want electronic invitation which can be emailed to you immediately.

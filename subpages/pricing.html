Refund/Cancellation Policy

A valid credit or debit card is required to pay your Jett Fast Rolling Video Games reservation. NO REFUND in the event of a reservation being cancelled within 14 days of your event. Jett Fast Rolling Video Games will make every effort to postpone your event with no penalty to another date should cancellation be necessary.

Food/Drink

Jett Fast Rolling Video Games does not allow any food or drinks within the Mobile Gaming Theater. Please have your guests eat and drink either outside the theater or prior to entering.

Alcohol/Drugs

You may serve alcohol at a Jett Fast Rolling Video Games event or party; however, drugs of any kind will not be tolerated in or around our Mobile Gaming Theaters. Any observed use of illegal narcotics will result in the immediate termination of the party.
